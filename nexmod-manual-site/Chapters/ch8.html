Chapter 8: Function Keys and Behaviour Modes

The following function keys operate independently of the interface’s surface state. Their results are context-dependent and may not reflect user intention.

  • F1: Unlearn — Purges recent associative structures.
  • F2: Enter Softstate — Suspends boundaries between saved and active memory.
  • F3: Reattempt — Replays previous function with altered parameters.
  • F4: Clear Phantom Loop — Attempts to locate and terminate hidden recursive echoes.
