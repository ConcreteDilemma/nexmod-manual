Chapter 6: Initial Calibration Procedure

6.1 Follow Prompt: "Are You Still There?"

Do not respond aloud. Wait until the prompt disappears on its own. This may take several minutes. Maintain eye contact with the screen throughout.

6.2 Tap All Corners of the Device

Tap all four corners in any order. A low chime will sound after successful recognition. If chime repeats, begin again more slowly.
