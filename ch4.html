Chapter 4: Powering the System (First-Time Initiation)

4.1 Locate the Rotary Seam

Use thumb pressure until the inner latch clicks. The system will acknowledge silently.

4.2 Apply Downward Pressure

Hold for 4 seconds, then release. You may feel resistance; this is expected.

4.3 Wait for Confirmation Silence

Do not proceed until silence confirms the mode lock. If sound continues, repeat.
