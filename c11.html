Chapter 11: Firmware Dissociation and Scheduled Obsolescence

The device will begin offboarding after three years of passive use. This process is self-initiated and cannot be reversed. User input may be ignored or mimicked, depending on obsolescence depth.

Symptoms of Dissociation

  • Halving of memory sectors
  • Syntax drift and compression loss
  • False shutdowns or partial awakenings
  • Loss of time-index alignment
