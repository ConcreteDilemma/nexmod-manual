Chapter 7: Using the Modular Feedback Console

7.1 Do Not Speak to the Console Unless Spoken To

The Console operates on a minimal recognition loop. Speaking unprompted may initiate dormant states or recursive logic errors. Remain silent unless engaged by prompt or flicker.

7.2 Button Logic

Each button on the Console responds to cumulative input memory. That is, previous presses alter present behaviour. No two interactions will yield identical outputs, even if timed identically.
